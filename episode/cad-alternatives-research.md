# Episode: CAD Library Alternatives Research

## Context
Research faster/more efficient alternatives to Scan2CAD and text-to-cad for the 3D scan → CAD pipeline.

## Key Findings

### Faster Scan-to-CAD Options:
1. **Point2CAD** (CVPR 2024) - 421 stars, newer than Scan2CAD (2019)
2. **Open3D** - Fast C++/Python, good preprocessing
3. **PCL** - C++ high-performance point cloud library

### Faster Text-to-CAD Options:
1. **text-to-cad** (earthtojake) - 5979 stars, most mature
2. **Point2CAD** - Also handles text prompts
3. **CAD-Coder** - Code-generation focused

## Recommendation
Consider Point2CAD as a faster alternative to Scan2CAD (newer, CVPR 2024 vs 2019).
